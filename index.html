About

Built for the Egyptian corporate market, run lean by design

Vanguard Business Solutions is a corporate training company serving Egyptian companies that need their teams to perform better — in sales, service, leadership, and the day-to-day work of running an operation. Our ground operations are based in Cairo.

We keep the team small on purpose. Programs are designed and delivered by our own full-time trainers, coordinated by a lean operations and business-development team. That means the standards stay consistent, the contract stays clear, and you always know who you're working with.

Our copy, our pricing, and our promises stay concrete. We name the program, the delivery mode, and the operational problem we're solving — and we price it in EGP, up front.
